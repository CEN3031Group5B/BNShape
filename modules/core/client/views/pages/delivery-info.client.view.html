Delivery Info

1.  All orders are shipped First-Class or Priority Mail via USPS.  Tracking information is automatically emailed upon shipment.

2.  International orders take 10-14 business days for delivery.  Packages are only tracked until they leave the United States.

3.  Returns or exchanges are accepted within 30 days of receiving your order.  To request a return or exchange, complete our return request form.  Orders within the United States will receive a return label via email.  International orders must pay for shipment back to us.

4.  If you have any further questions, please email us at info@bnshapeclothing.com
